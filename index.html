About Mainstream Nails

"Beauty is when you can appreciate yourself. When you love yourself, that's when you're most beautiful."

Zoe Kravitz
Mainstream Nails was founded in 2013 when Melly successfully drew a design when one of her clients challenged her. Once she accomplished that, the possibilities were endless.

main·stream /ˈmānˌstrēm/ - "the dominant trend in opinion, fashion, or the arts." - was chosen as the salon's name, as it ties into the belief that nail art is huge part of the beauty industry's trends.
We have created a team that has a passion for nail art. We are one of a kind nail salon that prefers appointments so we can focus on our relationship with our clients but welcome walkins and look forward to joining our neighbor Whittier, Cole, Five Points, RiNo (wherever your area, we are so honored to be serving the 80205). Our goal is to become part of the Denver art community and bring art in a way that Denver has not seen, as the art community has impacted our vision to taking nail services to the NEXT MNSTRM LEVEL!
