About Mainstream Nails

"Mainstream Nails is a direct reflection of who I am -my work, my style, my personality. Live by who you are and let it show in your business. Be authentic, create what’s possible and cultivate your culture."

Melly Nguyen
Mainstream Nails was established in 2018 and founded in 2013 by Melly Nguyen. Being surrounded and influenced by hard working and independent women was a huge factor in creating Mainstream Nails. Curating the concept for the salon; she was determined to not only provide an aesthetically pleasing space but also one that set each and every artist up for success. She learned that there were things in nail shop experiences that required both imagination and innovation. So she applied any and all of those concepts during the construction of Mainstream, and continues to practice that regularly.
Recognizing that there’s always room for growth and being open to any hard work that it takes to reach optimum goals. Once she accomplished that, the possibilities were endless and succeeded the “MNSTRM” way.

main·stream /ˈmānˌstrēm/ - "the dominant trend in opinion, fashion, or the arts." - was chosen as the salon's name, as it ties into the belief that nail art is a huge part of the beauty industry's trends.

Why Choose Us?

We are one of the most prestigious nail salons in Denver! We are committed to high quality services, great vibes, practice clean and safe guidelines, and value in educating our clients. MNSTRM provides the most unique experiences. Whether it's a relaxing pamper day, a girl’s night out, or nail art that will blow your mind, we are here to show you a good time.

We’re Creative

What we do here is we ‘Keep It MNSTRM’ and take one’s imaginative ideas and turn it into a creative masterpiece. Our creativity is “fresh”, “innovative” and “Mainstream.” We offer a range of different artists’ that cater to all styles of nail art and match you with the best artist we think that suits your style!

We’re Punctual

We are an appointment based salon and do what we can to stay on time. Punctuality is a virtue that leads to great achievements in life. We stay as punctual as possible and do as much as we can to ensure we have completed the look you were going for.

We have magic

What makes us different from everyone else? Absolutely nothing. We are here to do our best and hope to make you feel as magical as you all should. Denver is and has become the next biggest nail art city and we are so honored to be a part with Denver's best nail artists.

We love NailArt

We have traveled all over the world to bring our clients the most exclusive nail products and provide hundreds of products to create. Ranging from swarovski crystals, charms, foils, pigments, glitter, watercolors, encapsulated art, stamps, decals, etc. The possibilities are endless!

We’re responsible

As a salon, we do our best to cater to everyone’s time, appointment, services and ensure that you are getting exactly what you wanted. We honor our commitments, we realize sometimes things don’t go as planned and engage to explore what options we have fulfill your needs.

We're Friendly

One of the biggest qualities at MNSTRM is that we are not just a team but we are a family and value everyone that comes into our home and treat them with warmth and respect. We have developed such an amazing friendship with our patrons and they are all so friendly that even as a new client you'll soon feel right at home.
